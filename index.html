Zodiac Cipher

Cipher Text and Image Converter

How to Use

  1. Enter the text you want to encode or decode in the text area.
  2. Set the shift value (1-25) for the Caesar cipher.
  3. Click the "Encode" or "Decode" button to see the output.
  4. For images, upload an image file.
  5. Enter the text you want to hide in the image.
  6. Click "Encode Image" to hide the text in the image.
  7. To decode, upload the encoded image and click "Decode Image."
  8. Decoded text will appear in the designated area.
